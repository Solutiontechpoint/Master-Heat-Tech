About US

Established in 1984 & Pioneers in developing Ceramic Band Heaters in India, Today Masterheat Industries is one of the leading Indian companies to manufacture wide range of quality Heating elements for Plastic & Other Allied Industries. Since our inception, we have established a strong foothold in the Indian & Overseas market.

With sound technical expertise, large resource pool & experience gained for over more than two and half decades our company continues to maintain & exceed standards in terms of quality, customer satisfaction and timely delivery that are demanded by the best of companies across the globe. A team of technically sound & dedicated engineers along with best in class modern infrastructure and qualitative inputs, Masterheat Industries is poised to perform an increasingly important role in polymer and plastic manufacturing sector.
